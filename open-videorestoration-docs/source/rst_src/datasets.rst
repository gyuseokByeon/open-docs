Overview
==========================================
Previous studies on video restoration are usually developed and evaluated on private datasets. The lack of standard and open video datasets restricts fair comparisons. Therefore, we provide here the information of the commonly-used datasets in different restoration tasks, encourging researchers to evaulate their ideas on a common ground and thus enabling fair comparison.
